# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 23, 2026

Alex Ticea ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the **Air Combat** mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection
**Air Combat** is designed to be a privacy-focused decentralized application (dApp).
- **Personal Data:** We do NOT collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
- **Blockchain Data:** As a dApp on the Solana network, your public wallet address is used to interact with the blockchain. Transactions are public on the Solana ledger. We do not store or have access to your private keys or seed phrases.
- **Game Data:** We may store local game progress on your device. Multiplayer match data may be temporarily processed by our game server (hosted on Render) to sync gameplay between players, but it is not linked to your real-world identity.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Solana Network:** For blockchain interactions and transaction processing.
- **Render:** For hosting our game backend.
- **Expo:** For application updates and performance monitoring.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

## 3. Data Security
Your private keys never leave your device. We use standard encryption and security protocols provided by the Solana Seed Vault and mobile wallet adapters to ensure your interactions are secure.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our official GitHub repository: https://github.com/alexticea/aircombat
